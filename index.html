Get to know me

2011

I started my programming Journey at the age of 14 in computer science class. This is also when I was first introduced to HTML and CSS and the concept of markup languages. Instead of signing up for a third Language in 8th grade, I decided early to step into the realm of programming.

2013

At the age of 16 I learned the basics of Java and Object Oriented Programming in general. Around this time my interest in the matter grew so big, I started experimenting with small projects on my own, such as making clones of retro video games I used to play with my Dad.

2016

After I graduated from school with “Abitur”, I decided to spend a volutary social year at the Kreisfeuerwehrverband Steinburg, doing youth development work. Since I’ve been a volunteer firefighter for 2 years at this point, the decision just came natural to me.

2017

At the end of the year I started studying "(B.Sc) Angewandte Informatik" at the University of Applied Sciences in Flensburg. Although I started with lots of motivation into this, it quickly became clear that a University is not the right learning environment for me. So I started getting more into learning on my own, until i eventually dropped out at the beginning of the COVID Lockdowns.

2020

In 2020 I decided to take a big leap into something, I've been dreaming of since childhood: I've Throughout my time as a producer I still kept using my skillset, be it creating my own cover art, or my online store to sell beats.

2023

At the beginning of this year I started to get back into programming and UI Design with the Goal of eventually getting back into the industry. I started with a couple projects on my own and compiled them all in this portfolio.
